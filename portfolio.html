Web-app Group Project
Imagine it's a Friday night and you're hanging out with friends and someone suggests that you all watch a movie. Sounds great, but how do you decide which movie to watch? GroupMovie makes this process easy. You can create a new virtual room and select which streaming services members of the group have. Once everyone has joined the room, voting begins. Different movies are shown based on the given streaming services with a search option included. Once the timer runs out, the top movies are put in a tournament and the group now has movies to watch that they actually want to see! No more wasting time trying to pick a movie that everyone will agree on, only to end up with nothing. Our app will make the process much faster and achieve results that everyone in the group is happy with.
